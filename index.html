Privacy Policy for Sober Motivation

Last updated: August 8th, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to Sober Motivation, an app designed to provide motivation and support for individuals seeking to maintain sobriety and make positive lifestyle choices. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Sober Motivation app. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your information in compliance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

Information We Collect

  • Optional Instagram Handle: Sober Motivation collects an optional Instagram handle from users. This handle is used to personalize and associate hourly push notification quotes with your Instagram account.
  • Optional Background Location Tracking: Sober Motivation offers an optional feature that utilizes background location tracking when the app is not in use. This feature is designed to determine if a user is near a bar or liquor store. If enabled, this information is used to send push notifications aimed at providing motivational content to support sobriety efforts. You can choose to enable or disable this feature at any time in your app settings.

Optional Yearly Subscription

Please note that the app may introduce a yearly subscription in the future. If such a feature is implemented, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly to provide you with transparent information about the subscription model.

How We Use Your Information

  • Personalization: The optional Instagram handle provided is used to personalize the hourly push notification quotes by associating them with user's Instagram accounts.
  • Push Notifications: If enabled, push notifications may be sent to your device to provide motivational content and support for maintaining sobriety.

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Choices

You can control and modify the information you provide to us:

  • You can provide or remove the optional Instagram handle in the app settings.
  • You can enable or disable the optional background location tracking feature in your app settings.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to your privacy or this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

Email: support@sobermotivation.com

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on the app or by other means.

By using the Sober Motivation app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
